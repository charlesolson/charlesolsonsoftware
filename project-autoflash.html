AutoFlash Sodexo, 2014

While working as an accountant, I saw the opportunity to automate many bookkeeping tasks within Sodexo, a foodservice contractor. The AutoFlash application continues to be used throughout Sodexo's University operations.

What it is:

  • Desktop application that automates bookkeeping tasks within the Sodexo Universities division.
  • Currently in use on over 100 University campuses.
  • Robust GUI and built-in new user tutorial.
  • Implements corporate governance features and allows user customization of accounting operations.
  • Automatic updates over SFTP through custom-designed Box.com API.

User testimonials:

  • Northwestern University: “AutoFlash is a valuable time-saver for our account.  We manage $34M, have six resident dining halls and 24 retail outlets between our two campuses. AutoFlash is user friendly, easy to learn and adjust to our specific units.  The time saved for inputting WAEs is priceless.“
  • Western Illinois University: "I can say that the AutoFlash reconciliation takes one whole day out of the process when I was reconciling 14 units by hand weekly and on Monday reduces the amount of input time for me by about 3 hours – so I would say I save 10 hours a week with AutoFlash if not a little more. ”
  • University of Pittsburgh: "AutoFlash enabled my senior assistant, who was very skeptical when I introduced her to the tool, to complete the week flash entry process in less than half the time that it took manually. I am especially pleased with the reconcile process at period close and have found that a job that I procrastinated on longer than I should have is now a task that is easily and quickly performed.”
